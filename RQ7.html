Research Question 7

How do bus and subway traffic in New York compare?

Visualization

Insights

1. Subway Ridership is Higher in NYC: New York''s ridership for the subway is continuosly greater than bus ridership throughout the years.

2. Similar Pandamic Drop and Recovery: There was a significant drop for both bus and subway ridership during covid (late 2020). After both have a similar rate of recovery.

3. Bus and Subway Ridership Share Trends: Outside of the Covid Pandemic bus and subway ridership both have similar drops and recovery throughout the year around the same times of year.
